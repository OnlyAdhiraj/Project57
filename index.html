My favourite hobby

is

Reading story books!



Do you want to know why I love reading? (Read on to find out)

When we read books, we feed our creativity something colourful to imagine. We can envisage the characters as we see them. The pursuit of books leads to the exploration of new dimensions. It brightens us up when upset or worried. You are too engrossed in identification with the character to worry about anything. Did you hear the term addiction (Don’t groan! I know you hear it from your parents everyday but all addictions are not bad). Addiction distances and dissociates us from the world of books thereby ruining our creative faculty where we can experience a chance encounter. However, if our mind longs for something but it does not affect our mood if we don’t get it, we must carry on with it, it must be one of the best addictions to have! And reading books is always a good addiction. You sit on a chair but you are actually sailing across rough seas, meeting fairies in fairyland or trying to breathe underwater where we can experience a chance encounter with mermaids. These are the reasons I feel that the world of books is the best place to seek refuge in...

Thank you for reading till the end! I hope you enjoyed the paragraph... Oh! And before you go, do not forget to read a book!
